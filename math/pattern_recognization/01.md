
$$
A: \text{the car is blue.} \\
B: \text{she witnessed a blue car.} \\

P(A) = 0.15 \\
P(B|A) = 0.8 \\

P(B) = P(B|A)P(A) + P(B|\neg A)P(\neg A) = 0.8 * 0.15 + 0.2 * 0.85 = 0.12 + 0.17 = 0.29 \\
P(A|B) = \frac {P(B|A)P(A)} {P(B)} = 12/29 \\
